Buyer Confirmation Checklist

Size and Form

Confirm U15, U16-20, U21-25 or U26-30, and whether the buyer requires specific count tolerance, net weight rules or processing form.

Moisture and Glaze

Moisture, glaze, soaking or net weight requirements must be clarified before quotation and order review.

Documents and Market

Health certificate, certificate of origin, plant documents and label requirements are checked by product, partner plant and destination market before contract.

How We Handle Inquiry Review

For new buyers, we first confirm the exact buying requirement rather than sending a generic price. This keeps the discussion useful and reduces mismatch on size, moisture, packing and import documents.

  1. Buyer sends target size, quantity and destination.
  2. We check available size options and packing suitability.
  3. Document and label requirements are reviewed before any quotation or PI.
  4. Only after the requirement is clear do we prepare the matching offer for review.
