关于叮叮喵水产 About Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood

Yantai-based frozen seafood sourcing and export support — 基于烟台供应链的冷冻海鲜采购与出口协同

我们是谁 Who We Are

烟台叮叮喵水产 (Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood) is a specialized frozen seafood sourcing and export support team based in Yantai, Shandong Province — an important seafood processing area in China. We help overseas buyers clarify specifications, compare supply options and prepare export documentation.

We work with qualified partner plants and verify product specifications, plant documents, packing requirements and shipment details before each order is confirmed. Certification availability depends on product, plant and destination market.

We communicate in Chinese and English, helping buyers reduce misunderstanding around product form, size, packing, payment terms, shipping documents and inspection requirements.

我们的核心产品包括冷冻三文鱼、鱿鱼、鳕鱼、虾、鲐鱼、沙丁鱼、金枪鱼、罗非鱼、巴沙鱼及鱼糜；具体规格、证书和单证按订单确认。

我们的优势 Our Advantages

  • 源头匹配 — Match buyers with qualified partner plants 匹配合格合作工厂
  • 装运前检验 — Pre-loading quality inspection can be arranged 可按订单安排装前检验
  • 完整单证 — Export documentation confirmed per order 卫生证、产地证、提单等按订单确认
  • 冷链管理 — Cold chain logistics management — we coordinate freight 全程冷链物流协调
  • 双语沟通 — Bilingual communication — no lost-in-translation surprises 中英文无障碍沟通
  • 透明沟通 — Practical sourcing comparison and clear trade terms 采购选择和贸易条款透明沟通

认证与标准 Certifications & Standards

Certification documents are confirmed by product, plant and destination market 证书按产品、工厂和目的市场逐单确认

HACCP plant documents — when applicable
BAP/ASC/MSC status — checked per product
EU/FDA requirements — checked per market
Halal documents — available only when confirmed
Health certificate / CO / B-L — per shipment
Inspection photos / loading records — on request

目标市场 Markets We Serve

We support buyer inquiries across multiple destinations; requirements are checked case by case 按目的市场逐单核查要求

🇯🇵🇰🇷

东北亚 Northeast Asia

Squid, mackerel, salmon and other frozen seafood inquiries can be matched with suitable specifications and documents. 鱿鱼、鲭鱼、三文鱼等按规格和单证要求匹配。

🌏

东南亚 Southeast Asia

We check packing, shelf-life, price level and destination port requirements before quoting. 报价前核查包装、保质期、价格区间和目的港要求。

美洲 Americas

Tilapia, shrimp, surimi and other inquiries require market-specific compliance and documentation checks. 罗非鱼、虾、鱼糜等需按市场核查合规和单证。

中东 & 非洲 Middle East & Africa
