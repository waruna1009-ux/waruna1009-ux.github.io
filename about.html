About Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood

Yantai-based frozen seafood sourcing and export support —

Who We Are

(Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood)is a specialized frozen seafood sourcing and export support team based in Yantai, Shandong Province — an important seafood processing area in China. We help overseas buyers clarify specifications, compare supply options and prepare export documentation.

We work with qualified partner plants and verify product specifications, plant documents, packing requirements and shipment details before each order is confirmed. Certification availability depends on product, plant and destination market.

We communicate in Chinese and English, helping buyers reduce misunderstanding around product form, size, packing, payment terms, shipping documents and inspection requirements.

Our Advantages

  • — Match buyers with qualified partner plants
  • — Pre-loading quality inspection can be arranged
  • — Export documentation confirmed per order
  • — Cold chain logistics management — we coordinate freight
  • — Bilingual communication — no lost-in-translation surprises
  • — Practical sourcing comparison and clear trade terms

Certifications & Standards

Certification documents are confirmed by product, plant and destination market

HACCP plant documents — when applicable
BAP/ASC/MSC status — checked per product
EU/FDA requirements — checked per market
Halal documents — available only when confirmed
Health certificate / CO / B-L — per shipment
Inspection photos / loading records — on request
