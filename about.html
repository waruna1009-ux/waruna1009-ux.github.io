关于叮叮喵水产 About Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood

10+ years connecting global buyers with premium Chinese seafood — 十年经验，连接全球买家与中国优质海鲜

我们是谁 Who We Are

烟台叮叮喵水产 (Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood) is a specialized frozen seafood exporter based in Yantai, Shandong Province — the heart of China's seafood processing industry. With over a decade of hands-on trade experience, we bridge the gap between overseas buyers and China's best seafood processors.

We are not a faceless trading company. We are industry insiders who understand the entire supply chain — from fishing vessel to cold storage to container loading. Every product we handle passes through HACCP and BAP-certified facilities with full traceability.

Our clients range from Japanese trading houses to European distributors to Southeast Asian food manufacturers. We speak your language — both literally (Chinese & English) and in business terms.

我们的客户遍布日本、韩国、东南亚、中东、欧洲及美国。我们的核心产品包括冷冻三文鱼、鱿鱼、鳕鱼、虾、金枪鱼、罗非鱼、巴沙鱼及鱼糜。
