About Dingdingmiao Seafood

10+ years connecting global buyers with premium Chinese seafood

Who We Are

Yantai Dingdingmiao Seafood is a specialized frozen seafood exporter based in Yantai, Shandong Province — the heart of China's seafood processing industry. With over a decade of hands-on trade experience, we bridge the gap between overseas buyers and China's best seafood processors.

We are not a faceless trading company. We are industry insiders who understand the entire supply chain — from fishing vessel to cold storage to container loading. Every product we handle passes through HACCP and BAP-certified facilities with full traceability.

Our clients range from Japanese trading houses to European distributors to Southeast Asian food manufacturers. We speak your language — both literally (English & Chinese) and in business terms.

Our Advantages

  • Direct sourcing from HACCP/BAP-certified processing plants
  • Pre-loading quality inspection on every shipment
  • Complete export documentation (health cert, origin cert, B/L)
  • Cold chain logistics management — we coordinate freight
  • Bilingual communication — no lost-in-translation surprises
  • Competitive pricing — no middleman markup
